Business Club

Business Club is a good platform for students to practice their leadership and entrepreneurial skills. It is a club in which students work in group under their supervisors. It provides the students with real life experience about the world of business. The club works under the supervision of faculty members. The organogram of BAIUST Business Club consists of three manpower of hierarchy strength such as: Advisor, Co-Advisor, Co-Operation members.
